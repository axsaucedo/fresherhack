The event

HackaSoton is hosting an Amazing 3-hour competition where coders will solve small challenges in order to win amazing prizes!

What's the catch?

Competitors will have to go through shots, pints and cocktails in order to win - challenging your coding skills to another level!

We'll be giving away awesome prizes for the top 3 Hackers, as well as for other side challenges that we'll have throughtout the event!

If this doesn't convince your, at the end of event pizzas will be on us to celebrate this Epic evening!

Partners

HackaSoton is one of the most renowned Tech-Entrepreneurial communities in the UK! They strive to bridge the gap between techies and non-techies through events like the #FreshersHack, Startup Weekend, SVC2UK, between many others.

Fish On Toast is the Entrepreneurial Society of the University of Southampton. HackaSoton and Fish on Toast work together in many projects and share the same objective of connecting engineers with entrepreneurs to make the impossible happen.
